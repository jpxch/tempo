# Tempo Product Brief

## Product Vision

Tempo is a personal operations assistant for creative freelancers. It collects scattered information, organizes it around clients and projects, and surfaces what needs attention today, this week, and soon.

## Target User

Ray is a creative professional in the show, choreography, and entertainment industry. He works with clients, artists, rehearsals, notes, schedules, creative ideas, reminders, and project details across multiple apps and physical planners.

## Core Problem

Important information is scattered across planners, notes, journals, apps, AI chats, calendars, and conversations. Because of that, important reminders, deadlines, follow-ups, and creative details can fall through the cracks.

## Primary Goal

When Ray opens Tempo in the morning, he should immediately know:

- What needs attention today
- What is coming up this week
- Who he needs to follow up with
- Which projects have important deadlines
- Where his notes and project details live

## Product Role

Tempo should feel less like a generic planner and more like a personal assistant at his side.

## Version 0.1 Goal

Build a polished static prototype dashboard using fake data.

The goal is to show Ray the concept and ask:

"Would this help you if it were real?"

## Version 0.1 Features

- Morning dashboard
- Today section
- This week section
- Upcoming important items
- Follow-ups
- Active projects
- Quick capture box
- Recent notes preview

## Not Version 0.1

- Login
- Database
- Real reminders
- AI
- Invoice PDFs
- Google Calendar sync
- File uploads
